CALCULATOR

Brief information :

A calculator is a device or tool designed to perform mathematical calculations quickly and accurately. It's used to perform basic arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, as well as more complex functions like exponentiation, square roots, logarithms, and trigonometric calculations.
Calculators come in various forms, including physical handheld devices, software applications on computers and smartphones, and online calculator tools accessible through web browsers. They are widely used in various fields such as education, engineering, finance, science, and everyday tasks that involve numerical computations.
